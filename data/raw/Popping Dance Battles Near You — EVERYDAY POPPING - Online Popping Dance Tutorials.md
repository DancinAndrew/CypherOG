### **15\.** **Book of Styles  
****Washington DC, USA**

Popping Category

Book of Styles began in 2012 to preserve and cultivate the traditions of Boogaloo/Popping through research and education of past, present, and future communities. What makes this event special is that it challenges dancers to battle using specific dance styles (tutting, footwork, robot, wave, etc.), encouraging them to master the fundamentals and push each other to explore these styles deeply. This approach fosters innovative dance forms and brings out the best in the participants.

**Link:** [Book Of Styles](https://www.funkinfocus.co/bos)

### **17\.** **Out Of the Shadows****  
Chicago, USA**

Popping Category

Out of the Shadows is the premier popping event in the Midwest. The event serves as an authentic representation of Black American culture and its impact, facilitating cultural exchange across diverse regions. With an emphasis on elevating the Midwest and Chicago popping scene, Out of the Shadows provides a pivotal platform for local artists and acts as a gateway for the public to learn about the rich culture of American street dance styles. More than a mere spectacle, the event is an immersive experience where attendees are active participants surrounded by dance, music, and art.

**Link:** [Out of the Shadows](https://www.shadowpuppetzcrew.com/events/out-of-the-shadows-vol-ii)
