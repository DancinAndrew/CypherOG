Red Bull Dance Your Style is an international mixed-style dance competition. The twist? The crowd decides who wins by voting for their favorite dancers. The 2024 season concluded with the Red Bull Dance Your Style World Final in Mumbai, where MT Pop took the title. The Red Bull Dance Your Style World Final 2025 will go down on October 11, 2025.
