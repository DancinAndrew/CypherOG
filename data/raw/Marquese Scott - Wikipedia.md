**Marquese Scott** (born November 5, 1981) is an American [popping animation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Popping "Popping") dancer. His work has been seen in music videos that he creates, TV shows such as _[The Ellen DeGeneres Show](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Ellen_DeGeneres_Show "The Ellen DeGeneres Show")_ and "[El Hormiguero](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Hormiguero "El Hormiguero")", advertisements, and live performances at award shows. He has signed with XCel Talent Agency and is part of the dance crew Dragon House. His YouTube channel is **WHZGUD2**, which includes many dances. His dance videos include Higher, [Set Fire To The Rain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Set_Fire_To_The_Rain "Set Fire To The Rain"), [Time Control](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_Control "Time Control"), [Wake Me Up](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wake_Me_Up_EP "Wake Me Up EP"), Fresh, [Hanging On](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hanging_On "Hanging On"), and his most popular, [Pumped Up Kicks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pumped_Up_Kicks "Pumped Up Kicks").

Scott was born in [Inglewood, California](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inglewood,_California "Inglewood, California").<sup id="cite_ref-MARQUESE_SCOTT_{{!}}_DOCUMENTARY_1-0"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-MARQUESE_SCOTT_{{!}}_DOCUMENTARY-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> He was first exposed to dancing when he noticed a dance contest happening at a local roller skating rink. Scott began to take dancing seriously while attending [North Central High school](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Central_High_School_(Indianapolis) "North Central High School (Indianapolis)") in [Indianapolis, Indiana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indianapolis,_Indiana "Indianapolis, Indiana"), joining friends in his school dance club. After high school he joined the military and continued to dance whenever he got the opportunity.<sup id="cite_ref-ca.news.yahoo_2-0"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-ca.news.yahoo-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-forbes_3-0"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-forbes-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> After his time in the [Navy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Navy "United States Navy"), Scott moved to [Atlanta, Georgia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlanta,_Georgia "Atlanta, Georgia") to be with his family. There, he met other dancers, learned the technique of animation,<sup id="cite_ref-MARQUESE_SCOTT_{{!}}_DOCUMENTARY_1-1"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-MARQUESE_SCOTT_{{!}}_DOCUMENTARY-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> and eventually began uploading videos of himself dancing to YouTube.<sup id="cite_ref-forbes_3-1"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-forbes-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-mememachine.viralvideochart_4-0"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-mememachine.viralvideochart-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> Scott worked at a local [Wal-Mart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wal-Mart "Wal-Mart") while he appeared on _[So You Think You Can Dance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/So_You_Think_You_Can_Dance "So You Think You Can Dance")_ and _[America's Got Talent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/America%27s_Got_Talent "America's Got Talent")_ with his dance crew Remote Kontrol. After Scott's rise to popularity he wanted to dedicate himself to traveling the world.

Scott has been a huge influence on people of all ages that have started dancing.<sup id="cite_ref-5"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> Scott's 53rd YouTube upload, a video of himself in 2011 dancing to a Butch Clancy remix of [Foster the People](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foster_the_People "Foster the People")'s [Pumped Up Kicks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pumped_Up_Kicks "Pumped Up Kicks"), is what propelled him to stardom. The Pumped Up Kicks dubstep remix went [viral](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Viral_video "Viral video"), gaining 1.5 million views in four days and over five million in one week.<sup id="cite_ref-ca.news.yahoo_2-1"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-ca.news.yahoo-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> The viral video currently has over 145 million views. All his videos on his WHZGUD2 channel currently have a combined viewing total of over 360 million with 2.38 million YouTube subscribers. Scott is one of the most famous street dancers according to the blog _CuriosityHuman_.<sup id="cite_ref-6"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup> In September 2019, he co-directed and appeared in the music video for _The Poetic Flow (Chill Hop Music)_ by independent artist _E-Styles ft Mark Fuego_ alongside several Venice Beach street dancers and roller skate performers.<sup id="cite_ref-7"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup>

Scott has often stated that the proudest moment of his career was dancing live at [El Hormiguero](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Hormiguero "El Hormiguero").

Scott dances to [dubstep](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dubstep "Dubstep"), a type of music that originated in [London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London "London"), [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom "United Kingdom"), and his animation incorporates the elements of waving, gliding, tutting and popping. He then takes those elements and creates his own transitions, resulting in a unique dance style. Marquese has had a major role in fashion as well. Scott often wears a wide variety of hats, jeans and often dances in Jordan sneakers.<sup id="cite_ref-forbes_3-2"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-forbes-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> While it has been said that he "moves in ways that don’t seem real",<sup id="cite_ref-ca.news.yahoo_2-2"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-ca.news.yahoo-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> his videos are not aided by trick photography. Scott reflects his body movements in real time,<sup id="cite_ref-ca.news.yahoo_2-3"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-ca.news.yahoo-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> and are un-choreographed.<sup id="cite_ref-forbes_3-3"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-forbes-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-pcn_8-0"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-pcn-8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup> Following the viral success of the Pumped Up Kicks video, Scott appeared on _[The Ellen DeGeneres Show](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Ellen_DeGeneres_Show "The Ellen DeGeneres Show")_ on two occasions.<sup id="cite_ref-forbes_3-4"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-forbes-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> He continues to do work with television and online advertisements that include companies ranging from Coca-Cola to Ferrari. Scott's video locations are uniquely chosen. He is known for capturing quality background elements within his videos such as nature, cities, and buildings.<sup id="cite_ref-mememachine.viralvideochart_4-1"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-mememachine.viralvideochart-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marquese_Scott#cite_note-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup> Marquese is the co-founder of the dance event in Atlanta called "Shut Up And Dance", a dance gathering of top dancers in Atlanta and around the country. He has performed worldwide and shot videos in numerous countries such as Singapore, Germany, France, Japan, the United Arab Emirates, Spain, and China. Scott also does collaborations with other dancers. He is currently represented by XcelTalent Agency out of Atlanta, Georgia where he lives with his family. Scott is rumored to have married his long-time girlfriend Lizz Monreal. He is not [Habesha](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Habesha_people "Habesha people").
