**Steffan "Mr. Wiggles" Clemente** (born 1965) is an American [street dancer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Street_dance "Street dance"), noted for his [popping](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Popping_(dance) "Popping (dance)") skills. He has been active as a dancer, actor, choreographer, dance instructor, music producer and [graffiti](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graffiti "Graffiti") artist. He is a member of the [Rock Steady Crew](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rock_Steady_Crew "Rock Steady Crew"), [The Electric Boogaloos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Electric_Boogaloos "The Electric Boogaloos"), Tribal Click, [Zulu Nation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_Zulu_Nation "Universal Zulu Nation"), and TC5 (also known as The Cool Five or The Crazy Five).

Steffan "Mr. Wiggles" Clemente was born in 1965 in the [South Bronx](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Bronx "South Bronx") area of [New York City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City "New York City"), of [Puerto Rican](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puerto_Ricans "Puerto Ricans") descent. He was raised on [hip hop](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hip_hop "Hip hop") since the early 1970s. His dancing career started by battling all around New York City. He built his battling reputation until he was able to battle around the world. This includes Europe, South America, Asia, the Middle East, Canada, and on the stages of [Broadway](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Broadway_theatre "Broadway theatre"). He has been credited for two feature films – the 1984 drama [dance film](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dance_film "Dance film") _[Beat Street](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beat_Street "Beat Street")_ and the 1983 hip-hop film _[Wild Style](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wild_Style "Wild Style")_ – which helped establish hip hop today. [ 1 ] 

During his time in the graffiti crew TC5, Clemente was known as "Rek" by other members of the group. Clemente originally got his stage name of Mr. Wiggles from a leader in the TC5 crew, who went by both "Seen" and "Mr. Wiggles". While cutting class in school, Clemente had shown "Seen" a certain dance move which inspired him to give Clemente his side-alias, "Mr. Wiggles". [ 2 ] 

Aside from dancing, he has worked in theater, film, and music with entertainers, including [Graciela Daniele](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graciela_Daniele "Graciela Daniele"), [Bill Irwin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bill_Irwin "Bill Irwin"), and [Ann Marie DeAngelo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ann_Marie_DeAngelo "Ann Marie DeAngelo"). [ 3 ] He has been a featured artist at the [Apollo Theater](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollo_Theater "Apollo Theater") (New York City) and performed at the [John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_F._Kennedy_Center_for_the_Performing_Arts "John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts") ([Washington, D.C.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington,_D.C. "Washington, D.C.")). He has appeared in music videos with musicians, including [Missy Elliot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Missy_Elliot "Missy Elliot"), [ 4 ] [Usher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Usher_(musician) "Usher (musician)"), [ 5 ] [Madonna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madonna "Madonna"), and [Limp Bizkit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Limp_Bizkit "Limp Bizkit"), [ 6 ] among others. [ 7 ] 

He, along with the members of Rock Steady Crew, was acclaimed at the first annual [VH-1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VH-1 "VH-1") [Hip Hop Honors](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hip_Hop_Honors "Hip Hop Honors") in 2004. [ 8 ]

- [Drama Desk Award](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drama_Desk_Award "Drama Desk Award") nomination for the [Ghettoriginal](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Ghettoriginal&action=edit&redlink=1 "Ghettoriginal (page does not exist)") production of _[Jam on the Groove](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Jam_on_the_Groove&action=edit&redlink=1 "Jam on the Groove (page does not exist)")_ [ 10 ] [ when? ] 
- [Bessie Award](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bessie_Awards "Bessie Awards") for Best Choreography for the theatrical production of _[So What Happens Now](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=So_What_Happens_Now&action=edit&redlink=1 "So What Happens Now (page does not exist)")_, a play dedicated to Buck4, a deceased Rock Steady crew member [ citation needed ] [ when? ] 

Mr. Wiggles's instructional VHS tape _Mr. Wiggles Sessions: Vol.# One \[_[sic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sic "Sic")_\]: King Tut Style_ was featured in an episode of [Red Letter Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Letter_Media "Red Letter Media")'s _[Best of the Worst](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Letter_Media#Best_of_the_Worst "Red Letter Media")_ entitled _Wheel of the Worst #17_. The episode ends with Rich Evans parodying Clemente as "Mr. Jiggles" who demonstrates "King Gut Style". [ 11 ] 

Clemente and his wife, Zoraya, have six children. [ 12 ] [ 13 ]
